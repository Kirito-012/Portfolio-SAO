About

SAO, Sword Art Online, The story follows the adventures of Kirito Kirigaya and Asuna Yuuki, two players who are trapped in the virtual world of a MMORPG game, Sword Art Online.

In order, to get out of the game, they have to clear 100 floors and only then, they can get back to the reality they've known.

Its a anime with lot of action, adventure and emotional, heartbreaking moments as well as comedy characters
